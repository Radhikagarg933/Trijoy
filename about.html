ABOUT TRIJOYS

Creating Games That Spread Joy

TriJoys is an independent game studio based in India, dedicated to creating fun, memorable, and easy-to-play mobile games.

We believe great games don't need to be complicated. Our goal is to build experiences that bring joy, laughter, and excitement to players of all ages.

From quirky arcade adventures to creative new concepts, every game we make is designed with one simple philosophy:

🎯 Fun Comes First.

Founded by passionate game creators, TriJoys focuses on crafting games that are accessible, entertaining, and full of personality.

🚀 Small Team • Big Ideas • Games Made With Joy
